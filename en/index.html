Micro-Expression Laboratory
MELAB

Micro-Expression Research Group, Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences

MELAB is a research team in China dedicated to micro-expression applications at the intersection of psychology and computer science, providing a solid foundation for micro-expression research and advancing the development of related technologies.

Micro-Expression Psychology × CS Applied Research

We also focus on intelligent human–computer interaction for mental health of people with disabilities.

Research Areas

  • Micro-expression: dataset construction, spotting & recognition, applications
  • Psychology × Computer Science: affective computing and behavior analysis
  • Intelligent HCI for disability mental health
